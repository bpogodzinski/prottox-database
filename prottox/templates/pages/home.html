About

Biological insecticides, consisting of insecticidal Bacillus thuringiensis proteins, are a good and safe alternative to harmful synthetic pesticides currently used on a large scale in agriculture and horticulture. The result of the work at Department of Computational Biology & Department of Microbiology at Adam Mickiewicz University is a publicly available web application and a database of interactions of B. thuringensis protein insecticides. The application not only allow for an efficient search of the collected information on the toxicity of B. thuringiensis proteins according to different search criteria (e.g. insect development stage), but also allow comparison of the results of different studies, e.g. to select the highest insecticidal B. thuringiensis protein mixtures with the highest insecticidal activity against a particular species of insect resulting from the synergistic effect of the components of those mixtures.
